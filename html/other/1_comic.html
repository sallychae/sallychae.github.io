"Tiny Ink": A small-scale Comic

ges·ture: (n.) motion as communication. (A thesaurus.com short defintion.) Duration: 2009 - ongoing Project: Personal, Comic Art Current: Instagram.com/tinyink.ppl

This figure study composed of graphite / ink (Formerly called "TinyFolk") involves a series of hand-drawn iterations of these human-like figures; with energy-sparked gestures and person-like expressions; that delves into the product of what a few, and connected, lines may say. It started as guilt-ful doodles on the sides of my lecture notes. The pieces now draw a fitting home in an art notebook; suited just for their upheaval and sure delight.   L (•◡•) / The finished characters size, typically, at 1" x 1". The little ink figures aim to pack as much energy and traits, into a tiny-square frame, as one stick-figure, certainly, can.
